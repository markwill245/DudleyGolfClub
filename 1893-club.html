Founding Member Initiative

The 1893 Club

Dudley Golf Club is proud to extend reciprocal golf privileges, under the foundational rules of the network, to registered members of fellow historic clubs founded in the year 1893.

Reciprocal Regulations

Arrangements must be booked strictly in advance through our Professional Shop or Club Secretary. Standard rules require players to present a valid home club introduction card and an active WHS handicap certificate upon arrival.
