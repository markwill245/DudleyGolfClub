Our Heritage

The Black Country’s historic hilltop course

Founded in 1893, Dudley Golf Club has stood at the heart of Black Country golf for over a century.

Built on tradition, shaped by generations of golfers, and influenced by the golden age of course design, the club continues to offer a timeless golfing experience.

Designed during the era of legendary course architect Old Tom Morris , the course takes full advantage of the dramatic volcanic ridge, creating a natural and enduring test of golf.
