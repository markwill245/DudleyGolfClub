Captain's Day - July 4th

Dear Members,

Your club Captain, Keith Woodall, would like to invite you all to his Captain's Day on Saturday July 4th. There will be a BBQ and some drinks on the course, along with a prize presentation in the evening followed by live entertainment.

  • Entrance Fee £5, including raffle ticket (2's payable in the pro shop)
  • Prizes for: Best Nett (1st to 3rd), Best Gross, Best Nett Front 9, Best Nett Back 9, Past Captains, Over 65's, Nearest the Pin on all Par 3's, Nearest the Pin in 2 on 18, Nearest the line on the 17th, and a Juniors 9 hole competition.

The Captain has requested that all prize winners attend the presentation in the Brian Clee Suite at Approx. 6pm, followed by live entertainment.

All Members are invited to play and please bring your friends and family on the evening and make this a Captain's Day to remember.

I look forward to seeing you all there!

Kind Regards,
Keith Woodall
Captain
